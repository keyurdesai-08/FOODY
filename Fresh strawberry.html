Fresh Strawberry

Fresh strawberries are a popular fruit known for their vibrant red color, sweet taste, and juicy texture. They belong to the Rosaceae family and are scientifically classified as Fragaria ananassa. Here's some information about fresh strawberries:

Appearance: Fresh strawberries are small to medium-sized fruits that typically have a conical or heart-shaped appearance. They are known for their bright red color, although some varieties can be yellow, green, or even white. The outer skin is usually covered in tiny seeds, called achenes.

Taste and Texture: Fresh strawberries have a sweet and slightly tangy flavor, often described as refreshing and aromatic. They are known for their juiciness and have a soft, tender texture

Nutritional Value: Strawberries are a nutritious fruit, packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. They are a good source of vitamin C, manganese, folate, and potassium. They also contain dietary fiber and are low in calories, making them a healthy choice for snacking
