Fresh banana

Fresh bananas are a popular and widely consumed fruit known for their sweet taste and numerous health benefits. Here's some information about fresh bananas:

Origin: Bananas are believed to have originated in Southeast Asia, particularly in the region of Malaysia and Indonesia. Today, they are grown in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.

Appearance: Fresh bananas typically have a long and curved shape. They have a thick, peelable skin that turns from green to yellow as the fruit ripens. Underneath the skin, you'll find soft, creamy flesh.
